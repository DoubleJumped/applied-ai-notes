Forward Deployed Engineering

Every company can buy the same intelligence — so the edge is where, how, and why it's deployed. Someone has to decide. That person is the FDE.

1

Audit

Map the real process, not the documented one.

Shadow full days beside the work. Trace every step one level deeper — the exceptions are the spec. Produce one operating map: current state, future state, one use case, boundaries, quantified value.

shadow full dayssystem accessoperating map
2

Build

One way it goes right. A thousand ways it goes wrong.

An agent built for the happy path is a demo. Add capability in order: loop, tools, guardrails, audit trail, structured outputs, checkpoints and resume, failure handling, retries with backoff.

guardrailsaudit trailresume after failure
3

Evals

Turn non-determinism into evidence.

A golden dataset of real cases, hand-labeled and graded: right data, required steps, matches expert, safe to act. Below 0.80 confidence the system escalates; high-risk actions always reach a person.

golden datasetgraded runsescalation rules
4

Deploy

Build on top of what exists — never rip-and-replace.

Integrate with the trusted stack, test in a sandbox inside their walls, and increase autonomy gradually: shadow mode, then approval on every action, then authority where it's earned.

integratesandboxearned autonomy
5

Observe

Watch production like it's your name on it — because it is.

Metrics, failure categories, cost per run. Every failure is categorized, investigated, and fed back into the golden dataset and the harness.

metricsfailure taxonomycost per run
6

Improve

Fixing one workflow exposes the next.

Bottlenecks are interconnected — the next engagement is visible from inside the first. The loop returns to Audit, and one workflow becomes the whole business.
